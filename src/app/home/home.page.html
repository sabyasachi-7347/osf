Our Humble Beginnings:

Back in 2022, we started with a dream to create a place that stands for good values, helping others, taking care of nature, and making sure everyone in the community feels supported. Triranga Ashram is more than just a place; it's a heartfelt effort to contribute to the betterment of society.

TIRANGA ASHRAM : Embarking on a Journey of Service Since October 2, 2022

Triranga Ashram began its journey on Gandhi Jayanti, October 2, 2022, with a simple yet powerful idea giving back to society. Inspired by Mahatma Gandhi's principles of service and community well-being, we set out on a mission to make a positive impact on the lives of those around us.

Trilok Dham Temple (A Spiritual Oasis):

At the heart of Triranga Ashram stands the Trilok Dham Temple, a spiritual sanctuary graced by the divine presence of Lord Ganesha and Radha Krishna. The temple serves as a focal point for spiritual reflection and devotion, fostering an environment of cultural enrichment.

Tribhuvan Parivar (Old Age Home):

Offering care and warmth to our elders at the Trivuban Family, we started building a place where the wisdom of our older generation is cherished, creating a home where everyone is family.It's a cozy home where our older family members are taken care of with love and respect.This initiative embodies the ashram's commitment to the well-being of every generation within the community.

Triveni Goshala (Cow Shelter):

Caring for the sacred beings, Triranga Ashram hosts Triveni Goshala, a shelter dedicated to the well-being of cows. Here, the ashram takes pride in providing a nurturing environment for the gentle creatures, emphasizing the importance of compassion towards all living beings. , we believe in kindness towards animals. The shelter is a haven for cows, ensuring their well-being and happiness.

Trishul Nursery Plantation:

The Trishul Nursery Plantation reflects our commitment to nature. We plant trees to give back to the environment, aiming to create a healthier and greener world.Acknowledging the critical role of environmental conservation, this initiative actively contributes to the preservation of nature, planting seeds for a sustainable future and promoting a harmonious coexistence with the environment.

A Heart for Service:

At Triranga Ashram, we're known for our dedication to service. Our team works hard to make a positive difference in the lives of people around us, living out the principles of seva and community encouragement.

Our Dream - (A Better World for All):

Our dream is simple yet profound, a world where traditions are cherished, people help each other, nature is protected, and everyone has what they need. Triranga Ashram is like a big family, and we invite you to join us in making our world a better and happier place for everyone.
